The Back Garden Protectors

Protecting you from Pigeons at any cost. Bark! Bark! Bark!

About Me

My name is Jordan Prescott I study Software Engineering at Manchester Metropolitan University. I am 22 years old so like most people born in the 90’s I now have an unhealthy addiction to technology. I spend way too much time on Instagram and Twitter. This doesn’t help my people skills, so I tend to get on better with computers and dogs.

When I was 10, I had got a new water gun and decided to test it out on my neighbors Yorkshire Terrier. What I thought would be a swift victory ended in a swift defeat. The dog decided he didn’t like being shot in the face and that he would bite mine. His teeth sank into my skin and one tooth into my eye socket. Nearly losing an eye you would think I would be terrierfied of dogs but in-fact I love them and own two, Bella and Lilly.

What Is The BGP?

This is a team of two pooches with one goal. To protect the garden from pigeons! They always keep a beady eye on the garden and when they see them, they shoot out the door barking away. All jobs are a success in shewing them off and letting everyone know about it. They are literally barking mad.

No pigeon is safe with these two around. Their motto is ‘Spot it, Spook it, Spoil me with treats’. Meet the team!

Protectors

Bella

Bella is a rescue dog who was saved by the RSPCA. She was left on the side of a road in a box with her siblings. It was a cold rainy day in the early months of the year when she was saved. She was taken to Leigh Cats and Dogs Home where I first met her, and she has been with me ever since. She is a mischievous, cheeky, lovable dog who’s favourite thing to do is play with her favourite toy and sleep.

  • Breed: Unknown
  • Favourite Toy: Yellow Ball
  • Job Role: Back Garden Protector from Pigeons

Lilly

Lilly was born on a farm. She is the youngest and the most spoilt. Like always the youngest gets what they want so she always has the best toys and treats. She is fussy with food, so she has a different kind of course and she is very needy. Her favourite thing is being warm. If you have a hot water bottle or the fire on, she is the first over to join you. Do not dare move when this happens, or she will steal your seat.

  • Breed: Westie
  • Favourite Toy: Pink and Brown Reindeer
  • Job Role: Manager of Back Garden Protectors (Self Assigned)
